Birth Month Flowers

January

The carnations symbolize admiration and love as the snowdrops symbolize hope and rebirth.

February

The violets symbolize modesty and faithfulness as the primroses symbolize young love.

March

The daffodils symbolize new beginnings and prosperity.

April

The daisies symbolize purity and innocence as sweet peas symbolize blissful pleasure.

May

The lily of the valley symbolize sweetness and motherhood as hawthorn symbolize hope.

June

The roses symbolize romance as the honeysuckle symbolize happiness.

July

The larkspur symbolize positivity and dignity as the water lilies symbolize purity.

August

The gladiolus symbolize strength of character as the poppy symbolizes imagination.

September

The asters symbolize love and affection as the morning glory symbolize unrequited love and morality.

October

The marigolds symbolize creativity and passion as the cosmos symbolize peace and tranquility.
